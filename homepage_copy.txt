Here at SixMonthLetter we believe that goals are very important, and we're not alone. If you want to make things happen you need goals. Lots of application help you get things done, but how do you know if you're making progress in the long term.

How can you remember what you wanted to have achieved just six months ago?

Simple

Write a letter to yourself at SixMonthLetter.

State what you want to have achieved by this time in six months.
In six months you will receive the letter from yourself via email.

Evaluate, rinse and repeat.

Remember your future.
